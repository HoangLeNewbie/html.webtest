Lamine Yamal

Lamine Yamal, born on 13 July 2007, is a Spanish professional footballer who plays as a forward for La Liga club Barcelona and the Spain national team. He is considered one of the most promising young talents in world football.

Career Highlights

  • Youngest player to debut for Barcelona in La Liga.
  • First player born in the 2000s to play for the Spain national team.
  • Recognized for his exceptional dribbling and pace.
  • Part of Barcelona's youth academy, La Masia.
  • Won the UEFA Youth League with Barcelona U19.
